Appointment Booking

How to Fill Out the Appointment Form

To book your appointment with Cure and Care, follow these steps:

  1. Choose your preferred department from the dropdown.
  2. Select an available doctor.
  3. Enter your full name and email for communication.
  4. Pick a suitable date and time for the appointment.
  5. Click "Make An Appointment" to submit your request.

We'll confirm your appointment via email. Contact us if you have any questions.
